Executive Summary
The Opportunity
Three Off-Market Hotel Conversions in Southern Europe and London
GRC Investment Managers presents Arax Properties with the opportunity to co-invest on the same terms as King Street Capital in three off-market hotel conversion projects operated by Room00 Group under Hotel Management Agreements. Room00 is a Next Gen Hospitality platform that emphasizes design and being a technology-enabled operator focused on high-occupancy, operationally efficient urban hotels and hostels across Southern Europe, now entering the UK. Lisbon is the lead deal. Bilbao and London complete a portfolio that reaches Arax's equity threshold of approximately €20M while diversifying construction and market risk.

Why Lisbon Leads

The building sits directly on Rossio Plaza, Lisbon's historic epicentre, with approved construction licence and full change-of-use for a hotel. Room00 already operates two assets in Lisbon, making this a platform deepening rather than a market entry. ADR is underwritten below the current submarket and occupancy is in line with other Room00 locations.

Why a Portfolio of Three

At €7M, Lisbon alone sits below Arax's €20M minimum equity ticket. Paired with Bilbao and London, the combined commitment reaches approximately €20M, on mandate, while spreading construction and market risk across three distinct geographies. This is also Arax's first hospitality investment, so a smaller ticket with diversified risk across a portfolio of three hotels aims to build capability and track record before deploying at larger scale.

Same Terms as King Street Capital: Arax co-invests on identical economic and structural terms to King Street Capital, including:

  • Origination fee: 1.5% of acquisition price
  • Asset management fee: 1.5% of total revenues per annum
  • Property management fee: 4% of construction costs
  • Promote: 20% above an 8% preferred return (hurdle rate)
  • Exit fee: 0.5% of gross exit price

Comparable Assets and Market Dynamics
Direct Comparable: Hotel Praca Figueira

The most instructive comparable is Hotel Praca Figueira, a 67-key boutique 4-star on Praca da Figueira, directly adjacent to Rossio Plaza, currently on the market with an approved architectural project at €23M asking price.

Key Comp Insight: Hotel Praca Figueira asks €23M with approved plans but no construction licence. Project 1 Dezembro acquires at €11.5M, equivalent to €5,910/sqm acquisition vs. approximately €11,800/sqm for a similar building in the same submarket. On an all-in basis post-capex, GRC IM invests at €9,860/sqm vs. an estimated €15,000+ /sqm for Figueira post-refurbishment , a meaningful discount on a like-for-like basis.

IC Considerations for Arax
  • Same terms as King Street: A rare access point into a King Street-backed platform at co-invest level on identical economic and structural terms.
  • Investor base diversification: GRC IM selectively includes Arax to build a more resilient club deal, reducing single-backer concentration for all parties.
  • IRR vs. mandate: London at 18.2% levered pre-tax is on target. Lisbon at 17% and Bilbao at 16.2% are a deliberate first-entry calibration with upside from conservative underwriting.
  • GRC co-investment: GRC invests 25% alongside investors in each deal. Interests are fully aligned.
  • HMA protections: All three HMAs include performance break provisions. Room00 can be replaced if the underwritten business plan is not met.
  • Platform access: Room00 is targeting 200 assets and €4B in investment. Early partners with demonstrated execution gain preferential access to future transactions.
